About This Project

Ever wished you could just talk to someone's resume instead of reading it? Well, now you can!

This interactive portfolio is powered by a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system that brings my resume to life. It's like having a conversation with my CV over coffee, minus the awkward small talk!

Ask me anything about my experience, skills, or projects using your voice or text. The system smartly fetches the most relevant parts of my resume and crafts a personalized response just for you.

Under the Hood:

ChromaDB for vector storage
Google Gemini for natural responses
Web Speech API for voice interactions
FastAPI & Python for backend magic
